# SME Outreach Messages — DBA Phase 6 Recruitment

**Goal:** shortlist 15–20 fintech SMEs (Nigeria + France), confirm 8–10 for August trials.
**Priority order (prof's advice):** 1) founders from the informal May conversations,
2) warm intros through your network, 3) cold outreach.

Rules of thumb: video first, no jargon ("technical and heavy" was the exact feedback),
one clear ask, and never more than one follow-up without a reply.

## Qualification checklist (before booking the discovery call)
- [ ] Fintech-oriented SME (payments / wallet / lending / adjacent)
- [ ] Nigeria-based, or Francophone-Africa-connected via France
- [ ] Already generating real transactional/operational data
- [ ] Uses **two or more** active digital systems
- [ ] Decision-maker (founder/CTO/ops lead) is the person you're talking to

## Discovery call structure (15–20 min)
1. **Their world (7 min):** what systems, who makes reports, how long month-end takes,
   what breaks. *Take notes — this is baseline data.*
2. **The demo (5 min):** play the relevant scenario from the video or share screen.
3. **The pilot (3 min):** free, 15 days, guided; research participation is optional and
   anonymised; agree start week.
4. **Close:** send the request-access link before you hang up; ask them to tick the
   research consent box.
