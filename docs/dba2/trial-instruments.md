# Trial Measurement Instruments — DBA Phase 7/8

Perceptions alone cannot carry Chapter 6. Impact = **after minus before**, so every
participant completes the baseline *before* their trial starts (capture it during or right
after the discovery call) and the post-trial questionnaire in the final trial days.
Platform usage metrics (sources connected, pipeline runs, rows synced) come automatically
from the app — record them per participant at trial end.

Administer via a form tool or verbally on the call; store answers against the participant
code (see recruitment tracker), never the company name.

---

## Baseline questionnaire (pre-trial)

| # | Question | Measures | Feeds |
|---|----------|----------|-------|
| B1 | How many separate systems or spreadsheets hold your business data today? | Fragmentation | Ch. 6 |
| B2 | How many hours per week/month does your team spend preparing reports or reconciling numbers? | Baseline cost (time) | Ch. 6, 8 |
| B3 | How many people are involved in producing a management or regulatory report? | Baseline cost (people) | Ch. 8 |
| B4 | How long does one management report take, end to end? | Report latency | Ch. 6 |
| B5 | How often do you find reconciliation errors or numbers that don't agree? (never / monthly / weekly / daily) | Error frequency | Ch. 6 |
| B6 | How confident are you in the accuracy of the numbers you currently report? (1–5) | Trust baseline | Ch. 6 |
| B7 | What do you currently spend per month on data, reporting, or BI tools? | Spend baseline | Ch. 8 |
| B8 | Which report or number is most painful to produce today? | Focus for the trial | Trial design |

## Post-trial questionnaire

| # | Question | Measures | Feeds |
|---|----------|----------|-------|
| P1 | How many data sources did you connect during the trial? (verify against platform data) | Adoption | Ch. 6 |
| P2 | Estimate the hours per week/month the platform would save your team. | Impact (time) | Ch. 6, 8 |
| P3 | Did the platform surface any errors or inconsistencies you didn't know about? What? | Data quality value | Ch. 6 |
| P4 | How fresh is your data now versus before? (same / hours better / days better) | Latency impact | Ch. 6 |
| P5 | How easy was the platform to use? (1–5, plus: what was hardest?) | Usability | Ch. 6, 7 |
| P6 | How confident are you now in the numbers it produces? (1–5) | Trust delta (vs B6) | Ch. 6 |
| P7 | How important is it that your data is stored in your country? (1–5) | Sovereignty validation | Ch. 6 |
| P8 | What monthly price would feel fair for what you used? What price would be too expensive? | Willingness to pay | Ch. 7, 8 |
| P9 | Will you continue after the trial? (yes / yes-if / no — capture the "if") | Conversion intent | Ch. 7 |
| P10 | Would you recommend this to another fintech founder? (0–10) | NPS signal | Ch. 6 |
| P11 | What single feature would matter most to your business next? | Roadmap evidence | Ch. 7 |
| P12 | The onboarding claim: how long did *your* first source actually take to connect? | Validates/quantifies the marketing claim | Ch. 6 |

## Platform-recorded per participant (no questions needed)
- Sources connected (type + count) · pipelines created · runs executed · success rate
- Rows synced over the trial · days active (logins) · features touched (transformations, insights, Ask AI)

## Cost log (for Chapter 8 — keep updating)
Track monthly: Railway hosting, domain/email, Resend/tooling, your hours (estimate
retroactively from February), any paid services. Actuals vs the Year 1 €30–40k estimate
is an explicit prof requirement.
