# Application Checklist & Action Items

---

## BEFORE YOU SUBMIT THE FORM

- [ ] **Complete HEC MOOC on Coursera** (if no existing HEC connection)
      Recommended: "Entrepreneurship: Launching an Innovative Business"
      Cost: $39–$79 | Link: https://www.coursera.org/hec
- [ ] **Decide on company name** and check domain availability
- [ ] **Create a logo** (PNG) — use Looka, Brandmark, or hire on Fiverr
- [ ] **Set up a landing page** with waitlist signup — Carrd, Framer, or Vercel
- [ ] **Build the pitch deck** (PDF) — see `03_pitch_deck_outline.md`
- [ ] **Conduct 3–5 customer interviews** with SME owners (Nigeria, Kenya, Ghana)
      Document their pain points, current tools, willingness to pay
- [ ] **Fill in all [YOU FILL] fields** in `01_application_form_answers.md`
- [ ] **Take product screenshots** for the deck and form
- [ ] **Record a 2-minute demo video** (optional but powerful)
- [ ] **Incorporate the company** if possible (not required but strengthens application)

---

## BEFORE JURY DAY

- [ ] **Practice the 8-minute pitch** — time yourself, aim for 7:30 to leave a buffer
- [ ] **Memorize the Q&A answers** from `04_jury_pitch_script.md`
- [ ] **Prepare a live demo** — have a working pipeline ready to run on jury day
- [ ] **Submit your deck** (PDF) the Friday before jury day as required
- [ ] **Bring valid ID + QR code invitation**
- [ ] **Do NOT bring a laptop** — they provide technology and wifi
- [ ] **Practice with someone** — get feedback on clarity, pace, and confidence
- [ ] **Prepare a backup** — if wifi fails during demo, have screenshots/video ready
